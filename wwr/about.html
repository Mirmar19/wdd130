History and Purpose

Our company was founded in 2010 with an idea to bring adventure and fun into the outdoors. We have always strived to invite others for the excitement that comes with rafting. We love the opportunities we have had over the years to meet new people and understand our communities better. This is a great opportunity to connect with each other and with nature.

We love to: have fun, be safe, and seek feedback so we can continue to improve our service as a top-rated rafting company within the state.
